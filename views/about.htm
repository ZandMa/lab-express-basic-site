About Leonardo DiCaprio

Born on November 11, 1974 Leonardo DiCaprio is an American actor and film producer.
Known for his work in biopics and period films,
DiCaprio is the recipient of numerous accolades, including an Academy Award,
a British Academy Film Award, and three Golden Globe Awards. As of 2019, his films have grossed over $7.2 billion worldwide,
and he has been placed eight times in annual rankings of the world's highest-paid actors.
